About BlueandCosmos

We build tools, data experiences, and a community that make space exploration accessible — while protecting the Earth we call home.

Our Mission

To bridge science, technology, and community engagement so anyone can explore the cosmos — and use that knowledge to advance sustainability here on Earth.

Our Vision

A world where every learner and explorer has the tools to understand our planet’s systems, plan for a carbon-neutral future, and reach for the stars responsibly.

Our Story

2024
BlueandCosmos begins as a passion project to connect space curiosity with climate responsibility.
2025
Launched the BlueandCosmos Dashboard with APOD, Science News, Live Weather, Celestial Events, and Launch Tracker.
Today
Expanding educational apps, lunar intelligence, and community experiences — open for collaborators and partners.

What We Do

🌍 Earth & Environment

Sustainability learning, climate-aware features, and pathways to carbon-neutral exploration.

🚀 Exploration Tools

Dashboards, live feeds, launch tracking, and hands-on data viewers for learners and enthusiasts.

📡 Data & Research

Integrations with trusted APIs and datasets; open resources for analysis and teaching.

🤝 Community

Forums, livestreams, and collaborative projects that make space a shared experience.

Careers at BlueandCosmos

We’re always looking for curious minds, creative thinkers, and problem-solvers who are passionate about space, technology, and sustainability. If that’s you, we’d love to hear from you.
